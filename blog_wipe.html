WORLD INITIATIVE FOR PEACE AND EMANCIPATION (WIPE) (since 1990)

by David | June 16, 2022 | Hot News

PRESS STATEMENT

WIPE is an international organization saddled with the onus of peace building, peace diplomacy, peace education and conflict resolution.

Our international envoys are working to ensure that world peace is maintained though affiliation with relevant international organizations across the globe.

Our current effort is however geared towards peaceful elections across Africa in which Nigeria is cogent and making a focus.

This however, necessitates appointment of peace ambassadors across the region with the view to sustaining peace.

Africa is a populous continent that calls for salvation without hesitation. While peace must be made a world accordingly to the motto of our organization "PEACE IS A WORLD".

Wishing Nigeria a peaceful transition.

Signed President
