We are Africa's Leading Magazine

Pan African international magazine is a leading magazine in Africa, It started 2010 and had held the best magazine position consecutively in Africa, and even after the advent of Internet and Social Networking sites our magazine have not lost it shades.

Like most Corporate Organizations, we had a humble beginning. The coming of Pan African International Magazine since 2010 into the news business market in Africa marked a new dawn in journalism practice. Since then, the company has not looked back.

Pan African International magazine changed the face of investigative and conscience journalism in Africa. Its editors set some cardinal principles for themselves, key amongst which was that they will tell their stories exactly as it is, and ensure balanced reporting by giving opportunity to all those involved to also state their side of any story. More importantly, they pledged to uphold the rights of the citizen and uphold the rule of law by crusading for equity, justice and fairness.

It is on this pedestal that Pan African International Magazine built a reputation for itself, It has become Africa's independent fortnightly news and general interest magazine. It is this same doggedness, integrity, and the upholding of the ethics of professionalism and good business practices (good corporate governance policy) that established the brand. The magazine stand on the side of the people and a torch to leaders in Africa for good governance .the Magazine is circulated across 10 African countries and all over the world on internet, we have a strong online presence, linking with other social networks sites. Most times the physical copies are bought off a week after production. This is where we need more funding and support to be able to publish millions of copies to meet the needs in the continent.

The magazines have board of directors, advisory board and editorial board that are responsible for decision and other management matters. The magazine is also registered to organized conferences and varied awards to deserving organizations and individuals globally.
