PAN AFRICAN INTERNATIONAL ANNUAL CONFERENCE AND AWARDS (PAIR-AWARDS)

The Pan African International Annual Conference And Awards, Also known as (PAIR-AWARDS) is a household name, an international event that holds annually in different countries in Africa to discuss the challenges confronting the continent, and proffer lasting solutions to those challenges, also honour individuals and organizations with different categories of awards, those who has brought notable political, socio-economic achievements to Africa. These awards are equivalent to any international recognition globally.

The glittering award night and conference focuses on discussion of the problem that are retarding leadership, good governance, socio-economic, political, educational, environmental, insecurity and unemployment etc. The conference spells out measures that can be used to present and promote Africa to attract investors, curb insecurity, promote development and create jobs. Different topics of discourse are selected every year, we have had various distinguished panelist who has done justice to the discourse in the past.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES OF THE AWARD/CONFERENCE:

  1. 1. To sensitize African leaders on the need to embrace good governance, accountability, integrity, and rule of law.
  2. 2. To promote Sustainable Economic Growth and Social-political development in Africa
  3. 3. To showcase and honour credible leaders, organizations and individuals who are contributing in development of Africa by honouring them with a prestigious Pan African International Award
  4. 4. To encourage leaders in various position in authority to see leadership as a call to service and as basis for sustainable development
  5. 5. To encourage leaders in the public and private sectors of the economy to embrace good governance
  6. 6. To create more awareness on the need of Honesty and Transparency in all levels of governance and leadership in Africa
  7. 7. To bring together African leaders, government institutions, research institutions, development institutions, investors, academic and private sector to discuss and proffer solutions to the challenges of unemployment, terrorism, insecurity and corruption
  8. 8. To discuss effective best practices of good governance and the impact that it plays in economic development in Africa
  9. 9. To show Africa's successes in other to encourage foreign investors who have an otherwise thought concerning the continent
  10. 10. To present Africa as the new bride of the world, encouraging unity, oneness and promoting united states of Africa, where one currency, one passport for Africa. Its achievable etc

The African Epoch Making event has always attracted prominent personalities, including all stakeholders in leadership positions, Captains of Industry, Governors, Ministers, the Diplomatic community, National Assembly Members, Secretaries to State Governments, Commissioners, Heads of Parastatals, Service Chiefs, Professional Institutions, Politicians, Council Administrators, Professionals, Academics, Non-governmental organization, Trade Unions, Religious Leaders, Traditional Rulers, among others. The ceremony is given adequate media coverage both locally and internationally.

The Annual Property Merit Awards/Housing Conference has become a forum that brings together all real estate stakeholders to discuss and display sector trends.
